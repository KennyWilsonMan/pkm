# Corporate Bond Default Probabilities

The possibility that a borrower, or counterpaty in a derivatives transaction that is obligated to pay us in certain sitatutions might default leads to the concept of credit risk. 

Rating agencies give ratings that attempt to categorize the credit worthiness of corporate bonds. 

## Cumulative Default Rates by Credit Rating (%)

## Sources

- S&P Global Ratings Annual Default Studies (1981-2023)
- Moody's Default & Recovery Rates Studies
- Typical industry averages used in credit risk modeling

A bond rates CCC/C has a 15% probability of defaulting during the first year and a 27% of defaulting within the first two years. Similarly the probability will not default in the first two years is (100-27) or 77%. 


The probability of default in the second year is 27-15 which is 12%.
